BIGAR WATERFALLS

The Bigar Waterfall is a most interesting waterfall, and is on the list of the 8 most unique waterfalls in the world. The water falls in little threads all across the face of a rock formation that looks like a mushroom.

(Update! In 2021 a portion of this mushroom shaped rock broke off, forever changing the look of this gorgeous waterfall)

MUNTELE MIC SKI RESORT

The Muntele Mic Ski Resort is just 15 min to the lift, or 30 min to drive the whole way to the top to the resort. It is an excellent slope to learn if you are a beginner, but also has steeper runs for the more experienced. Rental equipment is available at multiple locations up there. There are several different restaurants as well.

(Open only during the ski season) The ski lift passes are an electronic card, which you can buy, and use up as you want. If you still have points left on the card when you come home, you can go up at a later time and still use up those points. Check out the Resort website and slope conditions. Or see the fun others have had on Muntele Mic on their Facebook page.

The Muntele Mic Ski Resort is situated at the top of Muntele Mic, and the cabin is set at the foot of the mountain. It takes 20-30 minutes to drive to the top on the paved mountain road, depending on weather conditions. There is rental equipment available at the Ski Resort.

The deposit is due 14 days before check-in, and is then refunded 5-10 days after checkout, provided there were no issues. If you made your reservation through a traveling website such as AirBnB, the damage deposit will be handled through that agency.

The Muntele Mic Region of Romania has many ways to past the time with family or friends. If you are a history buff, you will love to visit Sarmizegetusa Ruins, Corvin Castle, Trajan's Bridge, and Turnu Ruieni. If you enjoy outdoor activities, there is snowboarding and skiing, hiking, rock climbing, ATVing and enduro, snowmobiling, bird watching, wildlife, and endless scenic drives. We often enjoy taking day trips from the cabin to visit the many Points of Interest Romania has to offer.
